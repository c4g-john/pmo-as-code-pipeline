## Purpose

Partners self-serve poorly today: portal task-completion is low and partner
onboarding is slow, driving avoidable support load. The business needs a
modernized portal that lets partners complete key tasks without intervention.

## Business Requirements

- **ATL-BR-001**: The business shall raise partner portal task-completion rate above 85%.
- **ATL-BR-002**: The business shall reduce median partner onboarding time below 5 business days.

## Out of Scope

Partner billing and the internal admin console are out of scope and tracked
separately.
