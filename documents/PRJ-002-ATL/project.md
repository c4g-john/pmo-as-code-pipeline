## Overview

Atlas modernizes the partner portal — a redesigned UI, a streamlined onboarding
workflow, and partner SSO — to raise task-completion rates and cut partner
onboarding time.

## Scope

In scope: the partner portal UI, the onboarding workflow, and SSO for partners.
Out of scope: partner billing and the internal admin console. See the charter
(ATL-charter) for detail.
