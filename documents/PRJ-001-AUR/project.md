## Overview

Aurora replaces the manual, ticket-driven customer setup process with a
self-serve onboarding flow, cutting median onboarding time from 14 days to under
2. It is the reference project for the full document spine — charter through
benefits realization.

## Scope

In scope: the self-serve onboarding flow, data migration from the legacy setup
tool, and the customer-facing progress tracker. Out of scope: billing changes,
the mobile app, and enterprise SSO. See the charter (AUR-charter) for detail.
