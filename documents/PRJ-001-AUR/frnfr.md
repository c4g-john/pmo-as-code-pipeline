## Overview

Detailed functional and non-functional requirements for the self-serve
onboarding flow and its progress notifications.

## Functional Requirements

- **AUR-FR-101** (traces: AUR-PR-014): The onboarding wizard shall validate all required fields before creating an account.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **AUR-NFR-05** (traces: AUR-PR-015): Progress emails shall be delivered within 5 minutes at the 95th percentile.
