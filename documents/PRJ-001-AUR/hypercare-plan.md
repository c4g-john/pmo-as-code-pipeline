## Overview

Heightened support for the two weeks following the onboarding cutover, to catch
and resolve issues quickly while adoption ramps.

## Support Window

Two weeks from go-live, 07:00–19:00 local on weekdays, with on-call cover
overnight and at weekends.

## Severity Levels

- SEV-1: onboarding is down for all customers — respond within 15 minutes.
- SEV-2: a major function is degraded — respond within 1 hour.
- SEV-3: minor issue with a workaround — respond within 1 business day.

## Escalation

On-call engineer first; escalate SEV-1 to the delivery lead and sponsor within
30 minutes if unresolved.

## Exit Criteria

- No more than 1 open SEV-2 incident.
- Fewer than 2 SEV-2 incidents over 5 consecutive days.
- Onboarding completion rate at or above 90%.
