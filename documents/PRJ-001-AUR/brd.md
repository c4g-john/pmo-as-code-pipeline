## Purpose

The business needs to reduce the time and support cost of onboarding new
customers, which today takes a median of 14 days and generates a high volume of
manual setup tickets.

## Business Requirements

- **AUR-BR-001**: The business shall reduce median customer onboarding time to under 2 days.
- **AUR-BR-002**: The business shall reduce onboarding-related support tickets by a material margin.

## Out of Scope

Billing changes, enterprise SSO, and the mobile application are out of scope and
tracked separately.
