## Overview

Tracks realization of the benefits committed in the onboarding business case,
against the Aurora charter's success criteria.

## Benefits

- Reduce median onboarding time below 48 hours (from 14 days).
- Reduce onboarding-related support tickets by at least 80% within two quarters.
- Improve 30-day retention of new customers by at least 5 percentage points.

## Measurement

Onboarding time and ticket volume are measured monthly from the product and
support systems; retention is measured at 30 days per cohort by the data team.

## Realized Value

At the two-week mark, median onboarding time is 1.6 days and support tickets are
down 78% — on track against target, with retention pending the first full cohort.
