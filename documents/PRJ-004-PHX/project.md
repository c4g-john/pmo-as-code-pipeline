## Overview

Phoenix cuts first-response time on customer support tickets from a median of 9
hours to under 1 by introducing tiered auto-triage and a follow-the-sun on-call
rota.

## Scope

In scope: auto-triage classification, the on-call rota tooling, and the
response-time dashboard. Out of scope: the billing support queue and any staffing
changes. See the charter (PHX-charter) for detail.
